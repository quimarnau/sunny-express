How to use Sunny Express search

  1. Select your trip.
    Type where you are or want to depart from. Then, type in which country you would like to go.

  2. Select your time.
    Enter your departure and return dates. Both should be whithin the 10 days limit of weather forecast accuracy.

  3. Select your dream weather:

    • Choose your temperatures interval.

      Slide to get a minimum of 10 and a maximum of 20 celsius degrees for instance.

    • Select your weather preferences by clicking on the icons.

      Click once means "I want"
       eg. implies I do want sun.

      Click twice means "I do not want"
       eg. implies I do not want sun at all.

      Else means "I do not mind"
       eg. implies I do not mind if there is or is not sun.

    • Precise your wind preferences.

      By default, it means you do not mind.
      If selected, you do have wind preferences:

      •    Slow wind - wind speed inferior to 40 km/h.
      •    Fast wind - wind speed superior to 40 km/h.

  4. Finally click search and here you go! Choose your destination, save your trip in your calendar and enjoy your holidays!
